# 📧 Email Marketing Optimization using A/B Testing & Uplift Modeling

## 📌 Problem Statement

Companies send promotional emails to increase customer conversions. However, not all users respond positively. This project aims to evaluate whether email campaigns improve conversions and identify which users should be targeted.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Measure the impact of email campaigns using A/B testing
* Evaluate statistical and practical significance
* Identify high-impact user segments
* Build an uplift model to target the right customers

---

## 📊 Dataset

Hillstrom Email Marketing Dataset (~64K users)

---

## 🧪 A/B Testing Results

* Statistically significant improvement (p-value < 0.05)
* Conversion lift ≈ 86.5%

---

## 🔍 Segmentation Insights

* New users showed the highest uplift (~169%)
* Multichannel users responded strongly
* No segment showed negative overall impact

---

## 🤖 Uplift Modeling Results

* Positive uplift users: 2,978
* Neutral users: 14,650
* Negative uplift users: 1,542

---

## 💡 Business Recommendation

* Target only high uplift users
* Avoid users with negative uplift
* Reduce unnecessary email campaigns to improve ROI

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

A targeted email strategy based on uplift modeling significantly improves marketing efficiency and customer engagement.
